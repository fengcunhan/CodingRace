# CodingRace — Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark GitHub-terminal aesthetic with a cyberpunk-lite edge: near-black blue-gray
canvas, a single warm orange accent that reads as "burning tokens," and a
finance-green for cost figures. Monospace for URLs/commands (developer trust),
strong sans for headlines. Motion is fast and confident — this is a leaderboard
flex, not a calm brand film.

## Colors

- `#0d1117` — background (near-black blue-gray)
- `#161b22` — card / surface
- `#30363d` — borders, hairlines
- `#e6edf3` — primary text
- `#a9b4bf` — dim / secondary text (brightened from #8b949e for AA contrast)
- `#f0883e` — accent (orange, "burn") — logo Race, #1 rank, flame, CTA
- `#3fb950` — cost green (USD figures)

## Typography

- Headlines / UI: `"Inter"`, system sans, weight 700–900
- Numbers: `tabular-nums`
- URLs / commands / code: `"JetBrains Mono"`, `ui-monospace`

## Motion

High energy. Entrances 0.3–0.5s, varied eases (power3/expo/back). Scene changes
are fast crossfades (0.4s). Ambient: breathing orange radial glow, subtle grid.

## What NOT to Do

- No purple, no cyan-on-dark, no generic `#3b82f6` blue.
- No gradient text (`background-clip: text`).
- No pure `#000` / `#fff` — tint toward the palette.
- No full-screen linear gradients on the dark canvas (H.264 banding) — radial glow only.
- No left-edge accent stripes on cards.
